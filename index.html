About Me

My name is Saeid NIKKHAH NASAB. I am a PhD student specializing in Geoenvironmental Engineering. My research focuses on the interactions between contaminants and soil properties, and I am passionate about sustainable environmental practices. I have published several papers in renowned journals and am actively involved in exploring innovative solutions in the field of geotechnics.

With a strong foundation in civil engineering and extensive experience in various projects, I aim to contribute to the development of sustainable engineering practices that address environmental challenges. I am dedicated to lifelong learning and continuously seek to expand my knowledge and expertise.
